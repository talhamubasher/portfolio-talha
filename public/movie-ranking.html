Talha's Favourite Movies

A Short List of Three

The Goodfellas

A well-written, cool and witty representation of NYC Italian mafia life.

Little Women

A novel that's been depicted as a film thrice, with the second attempt being the best with my favourite actor, Winona Ryder.

Dead Poets Society

Robin William's loveliest work, acting as a teacher, but really more than that. Especially popular with people who appreciate art and literature.
